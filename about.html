Background

I started my career as a management consultant where I specialized in strategy planning and executing operational initiatives for various Financial Services firms. My role included interpreting data, conducting quantitative analyses and research, and communicating recommendations to our clients. I worked in a fast-paced environment where priorities were constantly shifting and producing high-quality client deliverable was always expected of me.

I also had a chance to work as a part-time Product Manager at a startup. After a few months, my role quickly expanded to become a design strategist when I started to conduct a series of product reviews myself in order to better understand the user's behavior on our platform. Having gained insights into what users found difficult or unintuitive to use, I took the initiative to lead the design thinking for the app, revising existing user flows and continuously iterating on the app design after each beta release

This was a pivotal moment in my career because I found the process of gathering user insights, incorporating their feedback into the app design, and seeing an immediate impact on user experience both fascinating and effective, leading to my decision to pivot into product design.
